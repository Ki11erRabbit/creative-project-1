Oatmeal

So I was a software designer for a company and we all really hated our boss so some of us came up with a plan to retire early. The plan was to use our knowledge to take all of the fractions of a penny when deciding payroll for this company. Since since this money would hidden by a rounding error. Unfortunately there was a flaw and we accidentally stole a few hundred thousand dollars. I decided to take responsibility for the theft and wrote a confession along with a check with the money. To my amazement, when I came in the next day our office building had burned down. It was that day that I realized that I wanted to be a construction worker instead. So I love my life now and here is my favorite recipe for oatmeal.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of rolled oats
  • 1 Cup of milk or favorite substitute
  • 1 Cup of water (or milk or fave milk substitute)
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 seaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of honey
  • anything you want to go with it like nuts and fruit

Methods

  1. Combine oats, milk, water, salt, and cinnamon in a saucepan.
  2. Bring to a boil then reduce to low heat.
  3. Simmer uncovered for 3 to 5 minutes or untiled thickened. Stir occasionally during this period.
  4. Once thickened, remove it from heat to let it cool a little.
  5. Divide between 2 bowls, and split the honey equally between the two bowls.
  6. Add other toppings if you wish.
  7. Eat with a spoon unless you think you can eat with a fork.
